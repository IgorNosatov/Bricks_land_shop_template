Warranty and service

Restoration of the warranty card

  • If you have lost or damaged the warranty card from the manufacturer, you can send a free-form request to us at service@BricksLand.
  • Be sure to include your order number in the request and attach a photo of the receipt and a photo of the serial number on the product.

Where can I exchange or return goods

You can exchange or return an item within 14 days using the delivery service.

Return conditions:

  • To process a return, take your passport, receipt and packaged goods with you.
  • We will refund your money on the day the goods are returned or, if there is no money at the checkout, no later than 7 days later.

We issue a refund if:

  • Product was not in use and has no traces of use: scratches, chips, scuffs, no more than 5 minutes of calls on the phone counter,
  • Software has not been changed;
  • Goods are fully completed and the integrity of the packaging is not violated;
  • Labels and factory markings are retained.
  • If the product does not work, the exchange or return of the product is made only if there is a conclusion of the service center authorized by
  • Manufacturer that the operating conditions have not been violated.

More details: https://BricksLand/warranty/

In what cases the warranty is not provided the store may refuse warranty repairs if:

  • Safety of the warranty seals has been violated;
  • Mechanical or other damage that arose as a result of deliberate or negligent actions of the buyer or third parties;
  • Rules of use set forth in the operating documents were violated;
  • Unauthorized opening, repairs or internal communications and components of the goods were changed, the design or schemes of the goods were changed;
  • Warranty card is filled in incorrectly;
  • Serial or IMEI number in the product memory has been changed, erased or cannot be installed.

The warranty does not cover the following faults:

  • Natural wear and tear or depletion of the resource;
  • Accidental damage caused by the customer, or damage caused by negligence or use (exposure to liquid, dust, foreign objects inside the case, etc.);
  • Damage caused by natural disasters (natural phenomena);
  • Damage caused by an emergency increase or decrease in voltage in the mains or improper connection to the mains;
  • Damage caused by defects in the system in which this product was used, or resulting from the connection and connection of the product to other products;
  • Damage caused by using the product for other purposes or in violation of the rules of operation;

Products that cannot be exchanged or returned:

  • Foodstuffs, medicines and products, hygiene items.
  • Jewelry made of precious metals, precious stones, organogenic precious stones and semi-precious stones.
  • Selected non-food products: clothing and knitwear, perfumery and cosmetics, feather and down products, children's toys, toothbrushes, gloves, fabrics, underwear, hosiery, products for babies, manicure and pedicure tools.

The service department / Seller is not responsible for possible loss / leakage of personal data about the consumer, his family members, third parties in the product (device): on the internal memory of the equipment or on its media transferred for repair / service, complete with main product in case of possible reprogramming, replacement of device memory, replacement or update of system software.
